Navigating the Friction of Mental Healthcare

The Bee Well Architect report identifies the critical cognitive and emotional barriers that prevent individuals with mental health conditions from successfully accessing care. When the healthcare system demands high executive function, those with ADHD, CPTSD, GAD, BPD, and Depression are often left paralyzed by the process itself. This interactive dashboard outlines the problem space and the proposed roadmap for a more accessible solution.
